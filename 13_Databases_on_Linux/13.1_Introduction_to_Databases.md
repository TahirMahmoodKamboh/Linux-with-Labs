# Introduction to Databases on Linux

Databases are essential for storing, organizing, and retrieving data. On Linux, you can use a variety of database systems, both relational and non-relational.

## Common Database Types
- Relational (MySQL, MariaDB, PostgreSQL, SQLite)
- NoSQL (MongoDB, Redis)

## Why Use Databases on Linux?
- Stability and performance
- Security and access control
- Automation and scripting
